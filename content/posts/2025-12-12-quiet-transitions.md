Quiet transitions reduce fatigue. The content layer should avoid scaling or
dramatic movement; focus instead on a subtle dimming and a short, ink-like wash.

We treat motion as an editorial device. The system should be felt more than
seen, prioritizing continuity and readability over decoration.
